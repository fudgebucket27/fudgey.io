The 10,000

The 10,000 is an NFT collection that I have release on Sei. It is a free mint.

There are 10,000 numbers(not including 0) in the collection, with each NFT looking like below:

The number itself is in the middle. The numbers surrounding the innermost circle represent the prime factors. The numbers surrounding the outermost circle represent the divisors.
