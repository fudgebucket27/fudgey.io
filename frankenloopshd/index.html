FrankenLoops HD(Hand Drawn)

FrankenLoops HD(Hand Drawn) are my own NFT collection based on the images of the various Loopring Loophead NFT collection drops. I train a machine learning algorithm on the image data set.

Then after the training is completed, I hand draw each FrankenLoop HD based on the images generated by the algorithm.

Check out the FrankenLoops HD below, the final version is on the right with the inspiration on the left.

The main purpose of this collection is to get better at drawing!
